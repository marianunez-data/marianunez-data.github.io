Problem

Reactive vs. Predictive: Solving Revenue Decay in Telecom

Interconnect operates in a hyper-competitive telecommunications market. Acquiring a new customer costs 5–7× more than retaining one. With a 26.5% churn rate, the company faces silent erosion of Monthly Recurring Revenue. This system transforms behavioral data into actionable early-warning signals for Marketing and Retention teams.

Mission: Identify high-risk customers and trigger personalized interventions before churn occurs.

Solution

A production ML system — from ingestion to live scoring

A fully automated pipeline that ingests raw data, engineers features, trains a calibrated LightGBM model, and serves predictions via a FastAPI endpoint, with a Streamlit Cloud dashboard for business users to explore ROI scenarios in real time.

Every system decision, including the threshold, cost parameters, and capacity constraints, is grounded in business economics rather than just model performance.

Business Impact

ROI Simulator & Financial Outcomes

Instead of optimizing for standard metrics like AUC, I engineered a Cost-Sensitive Threshold (θ=0.41) designed to maximize Net ROI. By balancing the cost of intervention (Cr) against the customer lifetime value (Vc), the model achieved a +$14,520 revenue swing compared to the do-nothing baseline, all while staying within the company's 25% call center capacity.
